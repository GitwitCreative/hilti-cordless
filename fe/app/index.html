SF 8M-A

Built for ultimate perfomance across metal
applications.

"Pros told us that having a cordless drill that reliably matches corded drill perfomance
in metal was their dream scenario, but most were sceptical that anyone could really
deliver that level of power and perfomance. We made it happen with a self-tightening
high-grip chuck, a four-gear motor, and advanced battery technology that gives users
ultimate control to get the job done without sticking."

SF 10W-A

The no compromise powerhouse for wood.

"When you're looking to drill massive diameters or put huge screws into wood, you
need plenty of torque. The SF 10-W finally gives users a high-torque option designed
specifically for their toughest wood applications. People find it hard to believe that a
cordless drill can actually outperform corded, but everyone who has tried ths drill even
once gets it."

SID 8-A

The ultimate impact driver for the world's utility
poles and wood beams.

"We heard time and again from contractors who said that cordless tools would be their
first choice for utility and heavy-duty woodwork - if they could perform. Many of those
same users have given this impact driver the nickname "The Beast" thanks to its
extreme power and perfomance. And it wouldn't be a Hilti if it didn't also have
user-friendly features like a chuck and grip specifically designed to be used with gloves
on and an available drop protection strap designed for professionals."
